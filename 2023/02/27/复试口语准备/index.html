My name is Zheng Xiangyun. I am from Shandong Province. I am a senior student majoring in Information and Computing Science at Jinan University. My GPA is 4.27 (out of 5) and I am ranked 10th out of 81 students.

I love learning English, I scored 494 in CET 6 and 529 in CET 4, and I am capable of translating English literature.

During my undergraduate studies, I have always strived to study hard and pursue advancement. Under the undergraduate multidisciplinary training model, I have systematically studied major courses in computer science、mathmatics and computing. With a GPA of 4.27 in the first 5 semesters, I hope to gain admission to our university.

Due to my excellent academic performance and overall performance, I have been awarded a school-level scholarship every academic year. I am very interested in deep learning, so I have learnt a lot about them and read many excellent papers such as 《Attention is all your need》《Vision Transformer》《ResNet》and so on, I also did some project in this aera,including 《Study on abstractive Text Summarization Based on Transformer》,and 《Gender Classification of Face Images Based on ViT and ResNet》, from this two projections,i have found that i am really instrested in deep learning and want to further my study during my postgraduate years.

I believe I am ready to start a new academic career here, and with that, thank you for your time and attention.

什么是字典树？

A trie, also known as a prefix tree, is a tree-based data structure used to store and search for a set of strings. It is particularly useful for solving problems related to string matching, such as searching for words in a dictionary or predicting the next character in a word.
