专业介绍

My undergraduate major is [Information and computing science] ,an interdisciplinary field involving computer science, mathematics, statistics, and information science.During my undergraduate studies, I had exposure to various disciplines such as mathematics, computer science, statistics, and physics. This has equipped me with strong analytical skills and the ability to abstract problems, allowing me to tackle problems using a modeling approach.

自我介绍

My name is Zheng Xiangyun. I am from Shandong Province. I am a senior student majoring in Information and Computing Science at Jinan University.

During my undergraduate years, I ranked fifth in my class and won the university scholarship every semester. I am very interested in data mining, machine learning and natural language processing, so I have learned a lot about them and read a lot of excellent papers, such as classification and regression trees, Naive bayes, support vector machines, etc. In addition, I also implemented simple projects on ‘Machine Learning Based Used Car Price Analysis’ and ‘Sentiment Analysis Model Based Online B&B UGC Data Mining’.

To better learn these techniques, I chose to apply to Ocean University of China. I believe that I can achieve better results through the guidance of my teachers.

Thank you.

自我介绍

Good morning, teachers.

My name is Zheng Xiangyun. I am from Shandong Province. I am a senior student majoring in Information and Computing Science at Jinan University. My GPA is 4.27 (out of 5) and I am ranked 10th out of 81 students.

I love learning English, I scored 494 in CET 6 and 520 in CET 4, and I am capable of translating English literature.

During my undergraduate studies, I have always strived to study hard and pursue advancement. Under the undergraduate multidisciplinary training model, I have systematically studied major courses in computing. With a GPA of 4.27 in the first 5 semesters, I hope to gain admission to our university.

Due to my excellent academic performance and overall performance, I have been awarded a school-level scholarship every academic year. I am very interested in data mining, machine learning and natural language processing, so I have learnt a lot about them and read many excellent papers such as classification and regression trees, Naive bayes, support vector machines, etc. In addition, I have implemented simple projects on “Machine Learning based used car price analysis” and “Sentiment analysis model based on online B&B UGC data mining”.

For my postgraduate studies, my research experience has inspired me to further my studies in the field of computational science, especially natural language processing.

I believe I am ready to start a new academic career here, and with that, thank you for your time and attention.

最喜欢的课程

One of my favourite courses during my undergraduate years was Data Structures. I learnt a lot from this course. It helped us to properly understand things (gain new knowledge), improve our logical thinking skills and it helped me a lot in understanding various types of algorithms.

在本科期间，我最喜欢的课程之一是数据结构。我从这门课中学到了很多。它帮助我们正确 理解事物(获取新知识)，提高了我们的逻辑思维能力，而且对我理解各类算法的帮助很大。

压力面试：没录取怎么办

To be honest, I think I should be able to pass the retrial, because I have good grades in my undergraduate degree and have some basic knowledge of algorithms. if I fail, It means that I still have some shortcomings . I must reflect on where I am having a problem and improve myself.

科研

①What do you think of doing scientific research?

First, we must have a large amount of knowledge reserve, in order to use the existing knowledge to solve the problem. Second, we must work hard and read a lot of literature. Finally, we must have a sense of innovation, in order to do valuable research.

首先，我们必须有大量的知识储备，才能利用现有的知识来解决问题。第二，我们必须努力学习，阅读大量的文献。最后，我们必须有创新意识，才能做有价值的研究

你为什么要考研

First ，With the rapid development of the country （and the popularization of higher education）, the social demand for individual knowledge will gradually increase., it is necessary further study.

Second ，studying in graduate school can improve my thinking and learning ability. It can also improve my knowledge and give me chances to make the acquaintance of many excellent people.，and I can have more options in my future

你为什么报考这所学校

First of all, Ocean University of China is an amazing school. The computer science program in this school is developing rapidly and the teachers in this school are excellent and have a lot of research achievements. I was attracted by the academic atmosphere of this school and studying at this university would enhance my academic background and personal skills.

Secondly, the location was another key factor that contributed to my decision; Qingdao is my hometown, where I was born and raised, and where I have family and friends.

围绕自我介绍的衍生问答

①介绍家乡

My hometown is a coastal city, located in Qingdao, Shandong Province. Qingdao is a city with a strong tourism industry and every summer, many people come here to escape the heat. I was born and raised here, and I have my parents and friends here. I love my hometown.

②介绍家庭

my family is very warm and harmonious there are four people in my family ， When I was young, my father often bought books for me, which formed my habit of reading . My mother often pushed me to study. I have a good relationship with my sister. Living in such a family, I developed an optimistic attitude towards life.

③介绍学校

My undergraduate school is university of Jinan , although my old school is not as good as Ocean University of China，it is still a key university,（重点大学） I appreciate it because it gives me a chance to improve myself. In college I learned to establish a knowledge system, which I learned from my teachers and classmates. At the same time, I not only gained knowledge, but also gained friendship.
